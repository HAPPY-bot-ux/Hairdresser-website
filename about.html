About Nelz Hair Dresser

Founded in 2014, Nelz Hair Dresser has become a premier destination for hair artistry in Cape Town. Our salon blends luxury, innovation, and warmth — offering transformative styling and beauty experiences designed to make every client shine.

  • • Over a decade of excellence in the hair industry
  • • Specialists in cutting-edge color and styling trends
  • • Eco-friendly and cruelty-free products
  • • Relaxed, modern, and hygienic environment
